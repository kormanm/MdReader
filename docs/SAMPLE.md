## Testing Instructions

1. **Test Zoom**: Use the zoom buttons in the toolbar
2. **Test Search**: Type text in the search box and press Enter
3. **Test Links**: Click on the links above (internal links open in same tab)
4. **Test Tabs**: Open multiple files and switch between them
5. **Test Persistence**: Close and reopen the app to see tabs restored

## Keyboard Shortcuts
